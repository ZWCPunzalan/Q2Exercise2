Momentum

Describes an object's "quantity for motion", it is a vector quantity, it's direction is the same as the velocity, and the SI unit of momentum is kg*m/s

Valence Electron

It is the amount of electrons on the outermost shell of an atom.

Force

Is an amount of energy applied that changes one's velocity.
